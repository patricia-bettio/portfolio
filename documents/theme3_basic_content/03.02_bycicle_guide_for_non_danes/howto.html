A survival guide to bike in Cph.

Hand Signals

Hand up for stop

Raise your hand up before you stop.

Sign at left or right

Give hand signals to the left or right before turning.

Important Rules

Overtaking

What over your left shoulder before overtaking
cyclists and always overtake other cyclists on the left.

Turning left

To turn left at an intersection you must first cross
to the opposite right corner of the intersection
where you stop and wait for the traffic light to
change before you go.

Ring your bell

A short ring on the bell is often a signal that a cyclist
wants to pass - so please keep on the right.

Light on

From dusk to dawn, bicycles must be equipped
with bicycles lights front and rear.

Do not ride against the traffic flow or on sidewalks/pavements, pedestrian crossings or pedestrian street!
Cycling is not allowed in parks in central Copenhagen!
There are traffic lights exclusively for ciclysts. Note when they 2, one being small and lower. If they exist, follow, otherwise, cars and bikes share the same.
Although not mandatory, the use of Helmet is strongly recommended - Safety First.
