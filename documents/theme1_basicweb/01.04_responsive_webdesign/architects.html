architects

Inspired by a philosophical movement, this post modernism style took shape and acquired fame through architecture. It was the discovery of freedom to manipulate materials and shapes, break it all, and bring in new, perplexing, insane, chaotic, constructions.In other words, a deconstructive architect is probably the biggest headache for any engineer.
What seems to be shocking is actually a controled chaos, precisely studied and planned. Sketches present calculated distortions, traditional structures being tear apart and its fragments rebuilt onto new and unexpected shapes, contradicting what our eyes would expect.
The intention is to reconfigurate shapes and break the rules, building structures thought to be impossible. Constant manipulation, unpredictability and some level of discomfort for the comon eye, represents this style.
